Fishing Charters Key West

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock cross-sell-main-tab3-content, type:text %}

Oceans Edge Key West invites you to experience the best fishing in the Florida Keys from our Oceanside Marina. The history of the marina spans an impressive 35 years and in that time we have been host to over 400 International Game Fish Association (IGFA) world records, which is more than any other marina in the United States. Our historic marina offers anglers of all skill levels fast access to a variety of fishing experiences, from flats to deep water, and even spearfishing. We've got the gear and the guides to get you in the water fast.
