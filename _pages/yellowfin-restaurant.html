Yellowfin Bar & Grill

Waterfront Dining, Key West Flair

A great advantage of our location is that you can bring your own catch to Yellowfin Bar & Grill and have it cooked to order. Our kitchen is expert at seafood preparation, showcasing favorite flavors of the Keys as well as Asian influences, mingling to bring out the best in freshly caught fish.

The menu is concise yet carefully crafted to appeal to a variety of guests. Start with clever cocktails, end with delectable desserts, and revel in the lively setting. Our restaurant was beautifully redesigned in 2019 to allow for indoor-outdoor dining at any time of year.

Oceans Edge is pleased to welcome guests for special Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s Eve meals.
