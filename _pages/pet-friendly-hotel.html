We Are a Key West Dog-Friendly Hotel

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock cross-sell-heading, type:text %}

Luxury for Guests and Their Furry Friends

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock cross-sell-main, type:text %}

If a vacation without your four-legged friend would feel incomplete, know that Oceans Edge Key West is a dog-friendly hotel that treats every member of the family to luxury. Your little furry ones will be met with a smile and treats at check-in.

We offer a waterfront dog park with convenient “dogipot” receptacles located throughout the property. Forget about booking a stay at the kennel and instead book a room at our oceanfront Key West hotel and enjoy a relaxing stay with your pet.

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock cross-sell-heading2, type:text %}

Dog amenities include:

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock cross-sell-main-amenities, type:text %}
  • Access to our dog park
  • Treats at check-in
  • Collapsible water bowl at check-in for you to take home
  • Dog door hanger to let our staff and other guests know that there is a VIP in your room
  • Doggie bags

Your pup is going to love Oceans Edge Resort as much as you will!

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock cross-sell-heading3, type:text %}

Important notes:

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock cross-sell-important-points, type:text %}
  • We welcome dogs weighing up to 50 pounds for a fee of $70 per night.
  • Our resort allows only dogs–unfortunately, we cannot accommodate other types of animals.

Kindly contact the hotel with any questions: 877-935-0862.

{% endregionblock %}
