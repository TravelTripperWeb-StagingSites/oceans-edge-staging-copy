Oceanside Marina is a full service marina at Oceans Edge Key West that offers everything to fulfill your boating needs. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be on hand to catch lines, assist with filling up, pumping out and more. Don’t forget to ask for secret fishing spots or secluded islands and sandbars to explore!

Fuel & Pump-Out Facility

Our brand new fuel and pump-out facility is conveniently located with an easy side-to mooring with over 200 feet of seawall.
Diesel and Non-Ethanol Gasoline
Open 7:00a.m. – 6:00p.m.

Wet Slips

Wet slips for vessels up to 140’ feet are available with 30/50 amp power, cable (Wi-Fi is also available), and water at each dock, along with a mobile pump out station.
